About

After years working in the film industry, John Robling became extremely disssatisfied with the long, unpredictable hours and lack of security that his life had been forced to endure, especially as he was about to get married. After nearly a year of soul searching, he finally decided that web development seemed to be an ideal career path for him.

He eneded up lucking out, as a global pandemic ended up shutting down all production for quite some time, enabling him to collect unemplyment while taking a full stack web development class at the North Avenue Trade School. After a week and a half of taking it, he is thriving in the environment, and is very happy about his choice and excited to see where this career takes him.

In his leisure time, he plays clarinet in the Seed and Feed Marching Aominable, a local street band based in Little Five Points. He also collects LEGOs, and has amassed a quite extensive and expensive collection since he started collecting again in 2015.
